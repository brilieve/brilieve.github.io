Life is in Our Hands

This collection of photographs is a dedication to hands. Often times, we all take our hands for granted and don't realize their importance. Hands serve as our passport through life, helping us get from one place to another...roadmaps guiding us through life. These photos were taken throughout the Spring of 2014.
