Life is in Our Hands

This collection of photographs are a dedication to hands. Often times, we all take our hands for granted and don't realize their importance. Hands serve as our passport through life, helping us get from one place to another. Our hands are a visual representation and roadmap guiding us through life. These photos were taken throughout the Spring of 2014.
